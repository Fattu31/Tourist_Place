Alto Vino

  • An Italian specialty restaurant, offering authentic Italian delicacies curated by Chef Christian Huber who hails from Italy. Chef Huber’s expertise is evident in the delectable variety of antipasti, pastas, pizzas, risottos, seafood dishes and much more the menu has to offer. A traditional wood-fired oven ensures that a guest’s palate experiences something unique with each preparation, enhancing the dish. Alto Vino also houses a Wine Bar with bespoke Italian wines including wine verticals and wines exclusive to Alto Vino like the Roberto Cavalli.
