OSHO GARDEN

  • In 1989, the Osho Ashram assumed ownership of the Osho Garden area. It partnered with the Shunyo Foundation, a Japanese environmental group, to transform it into the park that it is today. Osho Garden has seen various alterations and upgrades through the years.
